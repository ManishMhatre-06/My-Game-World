Game Description

Get ready for a frantic, feathery adventure in **Chicken Dash**! This arcade-style game challenges your reflexes and strategic thinking as you guide a plucky chicken through a series of increasingly chaotic levels. Avoid cunning predators, collect golden corn, and race against the clock to reach your cozy coop. With vibrant graphics and addictive gameplay, Chicken Dash is easy to pick up but hard to master!

How to Play

Mastering Chicken Dash is all about quick movements and smart choices.

  • Movement: Use the **Arrow Keys** (Up, Down, Left, Right) or **WASD** (W for Up, A for Left, S for Down, D for Right) to control your chicken's direction.
  • Objective: Navigate through the obstacles to collect all the shining **golden corn** in each level.
  • Avoid Enemies: Watch out for hungry foxes, swooping hawks, and other farm hazards! Touching an enemy will result in losing a life.
  • Time Limit: Each level has a time limit. Collect the corn and reach the **coop exit** before time runs out to proceed.
  • Power-ups: Discover special power-ups like temporary speed boosts or shields to help you on your dash!

Good luck, and happy dashing!
